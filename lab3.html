About Us

This organization is a legally authorized sports broadcaster and betting platform from Nepal. We own all the legal rights to broadcast the live streaming of various sports like Football, Cricket, Tennis etc. We are motivated with the intent of bringing every national and international games to people in every corner of the world. We have created various platforms for sports lovers to interact with each other, give opinions, place bets.

Our Services

  1. We organize betting for every game and provide rewards as per the bets.
  2. We provide live broadcast, scores, ratings etc. of various games.
  3. We offer expert analysis, insights and predictions on games.
  4. We provide a platform for discussions and fan engagement.
  5. We ensure secure and legal betting transactions.
